Role Based

Frontend Developer


Any aspect of a website, software package, or app that a user interacts with is referred to as the front end. Also known as the client side of the application, it encompasses everything that a user sees on the screen.


Backend Developer


The back end refers to parts of a computer application or a program's code that allow it to operate and that cannot be accessed by a user.
